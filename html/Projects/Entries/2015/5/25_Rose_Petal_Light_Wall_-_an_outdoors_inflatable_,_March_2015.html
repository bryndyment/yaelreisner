Rose Petal Light Wall - an outdoors inflatable , March 2015


In collaboration with Quim Guixa (Engineer, a world expert in making / manufacturing / installing inflatables).

See:    quimguixa.com 

  


The Rose Petal Light Wall is proposed for installing outdoors, as a light wall in gardens, easily movable or stored. That outdoors petal light wall has a different structure, and is made of different textiles.


The inflatable petal – a free standing object that lights its surrounding - was created by first capturing - through a 3D digital special scanning procedure - the tenderness of a selected live rose petal.



An on going project.
