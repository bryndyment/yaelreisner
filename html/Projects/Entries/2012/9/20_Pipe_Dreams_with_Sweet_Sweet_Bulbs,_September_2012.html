Pipe Dreams with Sweet Sweet Bulbs, September 2012

Thursday, 20 September 2012

By Yael Reisner with Dee Ferris


A 3D printed miniature fresco, a plaster relief mounted on engraved plywood.

Size: 42 x 80cm - The Garden Facade of our 'A Room For London' Competition entry.


The object was derived from the 3D drawing of A Room for London. We were interested in printing three dimensionally a painting made of plaster, a 3D miniature fresco printed in colored plaster.


We made this as a 3D printed piece, where the three-dimensional fresco - the entrance niche leading to the room – was embedded within the white elegantly curved garden facade. The object was mounted on a laser engraved plywood as its complementing background.


Exhibited in a group exhibition – A Few Friends - at TESTBED1, Battersea, Curated by Will Alsop,  as part of the London Design Festival 2012.


Opening: 20th September 2012.
