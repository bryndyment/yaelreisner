The Embrace Tables; Social Embrace, Embrace I+Embrace II – 2nd prototype, March 2014 

Thursday, 6 March 2014

The improved 2nd version of the Embrace Tables was designed by Yael Reisner with Peter Cook, following their 1st prototype designed and produced in London in July 2011.


The digital manufacturer Beyon3D produced it in Tel Aviv.  www.beyon3d.com


A contemporary alternative to the classic round or elliptical formation whereby we offer two tables that can be combined into a larger one. ‘Embrace I’ seats seven. ‘Embrace I+II’ seats ten. Ease of conversation is the aim: often fragmented by a rectangular ten-person table.

‘Embrace II’ is designed to function as a desk, when placed alone, thus all parts are always in use; an appropriate answer for versatile needs.

Each prototype  - with its different colour combination – is matching the plywood chairs by Arne Jacobsen.
