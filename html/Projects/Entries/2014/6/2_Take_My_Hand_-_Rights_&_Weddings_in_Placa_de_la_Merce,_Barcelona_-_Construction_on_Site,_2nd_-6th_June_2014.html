Take My Hand - Rights & Weddings in Placa de la Merce, Barcelona - Construction

on Site, 2nd-6th June 2014

See videos @

http://www.fundacioenricmiralles.com/?portfolio=bcn-re-set-democracia-3&lang=en


Designed by: Yael Reisner.  (See comment on authorship by Peter Cook under News - 1st june 2014)

Project architect: Yael Reisner

Architectural assistants at Yael Reisner Studio: Breanna Urquhart ,  Elin Schnipper,  and Ángela Bailén López.


Budget:  36,000 Euros.

List of collaborators on Take My Hand- Rights & Weddings:


Grup Roig, Barcelona  – Plants and flowers nursery. The technical director Ignasi Calvo, was in charge of the flower columns’ building and growth, two months ahead of the opening date, and their installation at Placa de la Merce,

See:  http://www.gruproig.com/


Quim Guixa , the Catalan well known expert in making inflatables for art display, objects, buildings parts, street festivals, theatre and opera sets.

See http://quimguixa.com/


Albert Mas Pérez of m·a·e – Barcelona. engineers and technical consultants – who were in charge of suspending and anchoring the inflatable element.

LEDs-C4 – All lights involved with the installation were sponsored by the Catalan light company, as well as consulting on lighting the installation.

See  http://www.leds-c4.com


David Juarez Latimer  - Straddle 3 – was the contractor in lead, constructing the installation on site with the Architects Collaborative Straddle 3,

See http://www.straddle3.net/    and with  http://arquitecturascolectivas.net/ 


Fundacio Enric Miralles, Barcelona - The installation in Placa de la Merce, (as all the other six installations), was coordinated, managed, produced and tremendously supported by FEM, and is exhibited at the FEM, along with the installations on sites, during summer 2014; 10th June until 11th September.

See: http://blogfundacioenricmiralles.com/2014/03/17/take-my-hand-placa-de-la-merce/
