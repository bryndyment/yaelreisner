Yael Reisner was invited with Peter Cook to design an installation in Placa de la Merce, Barcelona, by Benedetta Tagliabue, as part of the BCN RE.SET festival, TRICENTENARI BCN – initiated by the city of Barcelona - celebrating 300 years of a Catalan commemoration of individual freedom. 


Tagliabue (Principle Director of EMBT and the president of EMF-Enric Miralles Foundation) together with the Catalan theatre director Alex Olle curated the city interventions, to include seven installations in seven different locations at the old city of Barcelona.


Placa de la Merce was our allocated square and we were asked to refer to the word DEMOCRACY in our design.


As it was evolved Reisner led the design. See more about that by Peter Cook at the News section: ‘1st June 2014 - on Take My Hand’s authorship’.
