Another event was triggered by the book, Architecture and Beauty, Conversations about a Troubled Relationship, written by Yael Reisner with Fleur Watson, published by Wiley, 2010.

It was announced that the International Conference and Annual Meeting of the Nordic Association for Architectural Research
in collaboration with the Faculty of Architecture and Fine Arts, Trondheim, Norway, 25 - 27 April 2013, would be focusing on the theme entitled: Aesthetics, the uneasy dimension in Architecture.  

Yael Reisner  was invited to give a keynote lecture.


See link about the conference:   http://www.ntnu.edu/ab/aesthetics
