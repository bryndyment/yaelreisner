Lebbeus Woods in conversation with Yael Reisner

November 2004

I interview Lebbeus Woods as a preparation for the book I wrote with Fleur Watson: Architecture and Beauty, Conversations With Architects About A Troubled Relationship, Wiley, 2010. This first interview with Lebbeus took place in New York, in the apartment he lived, on the 16th November 2004, eight years ago.

This interview is here for all those who miss Lebbeus Woods, the thoughts,  as well as the voice.

Reisner is interviewed at UDK, Berlin, 2011

Architect, Dr. Yael Reisner  is interviewed before her lecture as part of the Monday Lecture series at the UDK, Berlin. The interview was run by students as part of the seminar ‘interviewing Architects’ taught  by Katrin Schamun and Georg Hana.
